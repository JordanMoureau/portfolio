Lots of little things add up

I love pork buns and bad weather. My name means a river in the desert and I think there's something about it that makes me chronically optimistic. I have these five subject notebooks that I fill with thoughts, plans, projects, goals, and lots of lists with little check boxes.

My special skill is building complex abstract things. Every project is a secret world I can sink into. Whether it's a book, a website or a design, to me it's a whole world. All my own.

designer & Developer

As Designer:

Design is like wooing. You have to make them burn for it, give them something they didn't see coming.

The next steps for me are gaining refinement. My strengths are iconography, UX/UI (I love digging into how people experience/use what I make), logos and websites.


Also as an aside, all of the taglines for my portfolio pieces are original. Except the poem. That's Emily Dickenson.

As Developer:

Adding the dimension of time to space is what UX/UI and web development is for me. Javascript is my new best friend, lately, and I'm super excited to start digging into paralax scrolling which is my next project.

I can always rely on programming to be a yes or no answer. Either it works or it doesn't. It's a comforting step out of the world of subjective art that keeps me out of my head.
