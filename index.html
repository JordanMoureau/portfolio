Lots of little things add up

I love pork buns and bad weather. My name means a river in the desert and I think there's something about it that makes me chronically optimistic. I have these five subject notebooks that I fill with thoughts, plans, projects, goals, and lots of lists with little check boxes.

My special skill is building complex abstract things. Every project is a secret world I can sink into. Whether it's a book, a website or a design, to me it's a whole world. All my own.
